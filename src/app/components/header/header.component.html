Essential Understanding

You can factor many quadratic trinomials(ax2+ bx + c) into products of two binomials.

Key ConceptFactoring Perfect Square Trinomials

a2 + 2ab + b2 = (a + b)2

a2 - 2ab + b2 = (a - b)2

Key ConceptFactoring a Difference of Two Squares

(a2 - b2) = (a + b)(a - b)
